Frequently asked questions

Will this actually get me students?

We do not promise guaranteed placement. We reduce wasted effort and increase hit-rate by better filtering, faster applying, and clearer commute fit. You still need to apply and win the assignment‚Äîwe just make that process less painful.

Sounds like more overhead. I'm busy.

Set preferences once. Daily shortlist comes to you. Designed for <2 minutes/day. The goal is to save you time, not add work.

What about privacy? Will you sell my data or share with agencies?

Explicit pledge: We do not sell your data. We do not share with agencies or tuition centres. Data minimization: we only collect what's needed for the features you use. You control what you share. In beta, we focus on tutor preferences and assignment details‚Äîsensitive student info is avoided unless required for a feature you explicitly enable.

Reviews can be unfair.

Verified-only, moderation, right-to-respond, dispute workflow (pilot rules). We're building this carefully to be fair and credible, not a free-for-all.

Agencies already exist. Why is this different?

Tutor Atlas sits on top of existing channels (Telegram, agencies). You keep your options. We remove friction and increase professionalism. Think of it as a workstation, not a replacement.

I run my own centre. I don't apply to Telegram assignments.

That's a valid different workflow. We can pilot a centre-mode track focused on enquiry capture, scheduling/trials, parent updates, and basic admin. If you still take occasional 1:1 or overflow students, the concierge can also filter for your centre's radius.
